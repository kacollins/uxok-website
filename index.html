FAQs

  • Where is the conference?

    We are at the Tulsa Central Library in the Pocahontas Greadington Learning and Creativity Center (Lower Level 2).

  • I’m new to UX. Is this conference for me?

    Absolutely. Our conference covers subjects for all UX knowledge levels.

  • Will I have a chance to network with other attendees?

    Yes! After the conference, we host an optional after-party for attendees to meet up.

  • What airport should I use if flying in for the conference?

    Tulsa International Airport (TUL) is our airport.

  • Once I'm in Tulsa, how should I get around?

    Uber & Lyft are both available in Tulsa. We also have scooters from Bird and Lime and bicycles are available from This Machine bike share.

  • What about hotels?

    Stay at the DoubleTree for a group rate, or stay at the Aloft Tulsa Downtown, Hampton Inn & Suites Tulsa Downtown, or the iconic Tulsa Mayo Hotel, which are all very close to our venue.

  • Where should I park for the event?

    We recommend parking in the Central Library parking garage, however there are many other public lots close by.

  • Will food be provided during the conference?

    We will provide coffee, snacks, and drinks thru-out the day, and lunch including vegan and gluten-free options (please mark any restrictions on your ticket registration form).

  • What if I cannot attend in person? Is there a livestream?

    Yes! This conference will be livestreamed. Please purchase a virtual ticket to get access to the stream on the day of the event.

Before attending the conference, please review the Techlahoma Code of Conduct. The Code of Conduct applies to all attendees, speakers, volunteers, and vendors at official and unofficial events by Techlahoma and any location where attendees may be congregating. Contact us if you have questions.
